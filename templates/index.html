Understanding Thai Coffee

From Mountain Arabica
to Southern Robusta

Thailand's coffee-growing regions produce strikingly different cups. Northern highland arabica from Chiang Rai and Chiang Mai offers floral, fruit-forward brightness. Southern robusta from Surat Thani delivers the bold, earthy punch of classic Thai coffee shop culture.
